About Me

Building, Learning, Creating

I moved from the USA to the Stockholm region five years ago for family reunification and have built my life here. At 51, I bring real-world experience, resilience, and a long history of working with people under pressure.

Before programming, I spent 15 years as a nurse, where teamwork, clear communication, and practical problem-solving were part of everyday work.

My interest in software started when I collaborated with a hospital development team, contributing user and workflow insights for tools used in real healthcare settings.

Outside of my studies, I keep learning through hands-on side projects, including home labs, virtualization, and cybersecurity basics, and sometimes share those experiments on my YouTube channel.
