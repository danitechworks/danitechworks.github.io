About Me

Building, Learning, Creating

I'm originally from the USA and have lived in the Stockholm region for the last five years. Before starting my journey into programming, I spent 15 years working as a nurse. That role shaped how I approach people, problems, pressure, and teamwork.

While working in healthcare, I collaborated with a development team on software for our hospital unit, helping shape the design from a user and workflow perspective. That experience sparked my interest in programming and showed me how valuable good software can be in real workplaces.

Moving to Sweden opened the door to a career change, and I chose to follow my interest in technology and software development. I'm now studying .NET and building a foundation in C#, web development, databases, and clean, practical code.

Outside of my studies, I keep learning through hands-on side projects, including home labs, virtualization, and cybersecurity basics, and sometimes share those experiments on my YouTube channel.

Danibot

Web-Based Chatbot with CI/CD Pipeline

A web-based chatbot project built to demonstrate a modern CI/CD workflow. The app answers common command questions for Linux, SSH, Git/GitHub, AWS CLI, EC2, S3, VPCs, subnets, and security groups.
